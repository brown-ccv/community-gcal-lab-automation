Reminder Calendar Rules

  • Source columns: B[1-4]STARTMIN10, B[1-4]STARTMIN1
  • Bursts: Bursts 1, 2, 3, and 4
  • Calculation: [Column Date] minus 10 minutes or 1 minute
  • Weekend shift: Saturday to Friday, Sunday to Monday

Retention Calendar Rules

  • Source columns: B[2-4]STARTDATE
  • Bursts: Bursts 2, 3, and 4 (excluding Burst 1)
  • Calculation: [Burst Start Date] minus 45 days
  • Weekend shift: Saturday to Friday, Sunday to Monday

Compliance Calendar Rules

  • Source columns: B[1-4]STARTDATE
  • Bursts: Bursts 1, 2, 3, and 4
  • Calculation: [Burst Start Date] plus 14 days
  • Weekend shift: Saturday and Sunday to Monday
